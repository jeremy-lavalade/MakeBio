MakeBio : What is it ?

Offer healthy food from local suppliers in a natural and eco-friendly place at affordable prices, in and for the world.

More than just a restaurant, MakeBio offers you an unique experience.

Our Values

Local

Organic

Affordable

Seven years ago, MakeBio's founder wanted to sensibilize and raise consumer awareness on their responsibilities on what they were eating, starting from an observation of the environmental problem. He wanted to show the World that we can eat good local food, fastly and without spending a lot of money.

More than just a restaurant, MakeBio offers you an unique experience in a natural setting, to chill with family or work with colleagues.

"Nature is our future, and we can protect it together"

With an innovative concept, offering local products and guaranteeing fair prices, all around the world, MakeBio wanted to implant themselves where the others never dared to go.

From the headquarters in Paris, France, MakeBio is located in 11 different countries. Each restaurant operates locally.

Today the 700 employees, all treated in agreement with our ethical and social values, are not afraid to stand out because nature is our future, and we can protect it together.
